Approach

1
Clarity matters in complex organizations.

When systems are large and responsibilities overlap, things work best when decision-making, accountability, and communication are clear.

2
Good systems support everyday living.

Much of my work happens in housing and large-scale real estate, where the systems behind the scenes shape how people experience daily life.

3
Learning improves the system.

I’m interested in practical ways people improve how they think and learn inside organizations. AI is everywhere now — sometimes real, sometimes just a label. Access isn’t the point. What matters is how thoughtfully people choose to engage with it.
